About

JSS Science and Technology University(Formerly known as Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering) offers various courses across different branches of Engineering which include Computer Science, Information Science, Electronics and Communication etc. It also has various sports, cultural and technical clubs which the students can join as per their interest.

Our Campus

JSS Science and Technology University is renowned for its state-of-the-art campus with modern infrastructure and facilities. The campus is spread over several acres and includes advanced laboratories, libraries, and sports complexes.

Student Life

At JSSSTU, student life is vibrant and diverse. We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, cultural events, and technical clubs. Our students are encouraged to participate and excel in various domains.
